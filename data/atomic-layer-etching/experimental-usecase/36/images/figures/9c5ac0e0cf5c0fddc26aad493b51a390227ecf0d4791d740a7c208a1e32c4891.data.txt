| Step | Description |
| --- | --- |
| (a) | TiO2 surface treated with C4F8 plasma, forming TiFx layer |
| (b) | TiFx layer reacts with CF Polymer, forming COF and CO2 gases |
| (c) | COF and CO2 gases are removed, leaving TiFx layer on TiO2 surface |
| (d) | TiFx layer is further treated with O2 plasma, forming TiFx layer on TiO2 surface |
