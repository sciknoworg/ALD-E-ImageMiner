| Process Step | Description |
|---|---|
| Ar<sup>+</sup> | Ion bombardment initiating the process |
| Cl Sputtering | Removal of Cl atoms by ion bombardment |
| SiCl Etching | Etching of SiCl complexes |
| SiCl<sub>2</sub> Etching | Further etching of SiCl<sub>2</sub> complexes |
| Si Sputtering | Removal of Si atoms by ion bombardment |
| Cl Mixing | Mixing of Cl atoms within the layer |
| Si Replenishment | Addition of Si atoms to replenish the layer |
| Amorphous Mixed Layer | Layer containing both Si and Cl atoms |
| Crystalline Si | Pure silicon layer at the bottom |
