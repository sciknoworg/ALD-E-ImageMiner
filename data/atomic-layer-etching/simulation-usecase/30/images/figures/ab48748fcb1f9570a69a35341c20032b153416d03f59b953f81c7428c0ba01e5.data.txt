| Step | Description |
|---|---|
| a) Starting Al<sub>2</sub>O<sub>3</sub> surface | Initial state of the Al<sub>2</sub>O<sub>3</sub> surface on silicon with native oxide layer.
| b) Partial fluorination | Fluorination process partially reacts with Al<sub>2</sub>O<sub>3</sub> surface.
| c) Excess fluorination | Further fluorination leads to excess fluorination of Al<sub>2</sub>O<sub>3</sub> surface.
| d) AlF<sub>x</sub>[O] surface | Surface after partial fluorination.
| e) CCl<sub>4</sub> interaction with AlF<sub>x</sub>[O] surface | CCl<sub>4</sub> gas interacts with the AlF<sub>x</sub>[O] surface.
| f) Removed converted AlF<sub>x</sub> layer | Etched material removes the converted AlF<sub>x</sub> layer, leaving Al<sub>2</sub>O<sub>3</sub> surface exposed.
